TERMS OF EMPLOYMENT

1. POSITION AND DUTIES
   Employee shall serve as Senior Software Engineer, reporting to the Director 
   of Engineering. Employee agrees to perform all duties assigned by Employer 
   and to devote full time and attention to Employer's business.

2. COMPENSATION
   Base Salary: $150,000 per year, paid bi-weekly
   Annual Bonus: Up to 20% of base salary, based on performance
   Equity: 10,000 stock options vesting over 4 years (25% per year)
   Currency: USD

3. BENEFITS
   • Health, dental, and vision insurance (Employer pays 90% of premiums)
   • 401(k) retirement plan with 5% employer match
   • 20 days paid time off (PTO) per year
   • 10 paid holidays per year
   • Professional development budget: $3,000 per year
   • Life insurance and disability insurance

4. TERMINATION
   This is an at-will employment agreement. Either party may terminate this 
   agreement at any time, with or without cause, with 30 days written notice.
   
   Termination Notice Days: 30 days
   
   Upon termination, Employee shall be entitled to:
   • Accrued but unpaid salary
   • Accrued but unused PTO
   • COBRA continuation coverage (at Employee's expense)

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
   Employee acknowledges that during employment, Employee will have access to 
   confidential and proprietary information. Employee agrees to maintain 
   confidentiality of all such information both during and after employment.
   
   Has Confidentiality Clause: Yes
   Confidentiality Duration: Indefinite (survives termination)

6. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION
   Employee agrees that for a period of 12 months following termination of 
   employment, Employee will not:
   • Work for direct competitors in the same geographic area
   • Solicit Employer's clients or customers
   • Solicit or hire Employer's employees
   
   Has Non-Compete: Yes
   Non-Compete Duration: 12 months

7. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
   All work product, inventions, and intellectual property created by Employee 
   during employment shall be the exclusive property of Employer.

8. GOVERNING LAW
   This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the 
   laws of the State of California.

Governing Law: State of California
